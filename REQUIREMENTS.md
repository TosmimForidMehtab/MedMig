Product Overview
Trinetra is an AI layer on top of ABHA. It is a mobile-first, voice-first, multilingual health records companion built for Indian families — primarily caregivers managing health for parents in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities. Hindi is the default and primary language; English, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu, and Tamil are supported in Phase 1 with a phased rollout.
Phase 1 ships a complete records hub: auto-fetched from ABDM, supplemented by OCR upload, organised by family member, made intelligent by an AI layer that explains, analyses, and helps share records with doctors. Phase 1 does not include medicine ordering, doctor booking, or lab test booking.
Core Capabilities in Phase 1
Onboarding with Aadhaar-based ABHA creation
Centralised records hub (auto-fetch + manual upload)
AI chat scoped to user's own records
Health dashboard with trends, observations, and cross-test patterns
Time-bound sharing with doctors (ABDM + secure link paths)
Family member management (add and manage records for parents, spouse, children)
Notifications and activity feed
Account and ABHA card management
